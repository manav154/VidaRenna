About Us

Vida Renna empowers young people to navigate family challenges and build emotional resilience through expert counseling and community support. Vida Renna is a team of eleven students working to bridge the gap between young people in need of family counseling and experienced counselors.

Our Mission

Vida Renna develops tailored roadmaps for each person, aiming at stemming family conflict, neglect, and abuse before things get out of hand. We are committed to fostering resilience, offering safe spaces, and facilitating access to resources that help them heal, grow, and thrive. Through our intermediary family counseling, we strive to create a brighter future where every young person can find stability, safety, and the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every young person, regardless of their family circumstances, is able to overcome adversity and build a life filled with hope, respect, and opportunity. Our vision is to create a community where youth facing family challenges are supported holistically, receiving the resources and care necessary to break the cycle of trauma, develop healthy relationships, and lead fulfilling lives.

Family Conflict & Support

Experiencing family conflict can be incredibly challenging, and we're here to help. Our Family Connection Program offers confidential support and mediation services to help youth and families resolve disagreements, improve communication, and strengthen relationships.

Our goal is to prevent situations that could lead to youth homelessness by fostering understanding and creating a supportive environment for everyone involved. You don't have to face these difficulties alone.
